AA Bungalow Kandy

Situated 1.4 km from Kandy City Center Shopping Mall, AA Bungalow features accommodation with a shared lounge, a garden and a 24-hour front desk for your convenience. The homestay features both WiFi and private parking free of charge. All units are fitted with air conditioning, and some have a flat-screen TV, washing machine, a kettle, completed with a kitchen. AA Bungalow offers a Full English/Irish or Asian breakfast. The accommodation offers a children's playground. Both a bicycle rental service and a car rental service are available at AA Bungalow. Bogambara Stadium is 1.8 km from the homestay, while Kandy Museum is 4.3 km from the property. The nearest airport is Bandaranaike International, 104 km from AA Bungalow, and the property offers a free airport shuttle service. We speak your language!
