Peradeniya Rest House

Peradeniya Rest House features modern rooms, each with its own private bathroom in Kandy. Featuring a restaurant and outdoor pool, this property provides free parking. The hotel is 601 m from Kandy Royal Botanical Gardens, 2.5 mi from Ceylon Tea Museum and 2.9 mi from Kandy Railway Station. Bandaranaike International Airport is 49 mi away. All rooms are equipped with a TV and private bathroom with shower. Select rooms are air-conditioned and come with a flat-screen TV. Towels are provided. A 24-hour front desk, garden and barbecue facilities are available at Peradeniya Rest House. Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 8.0 for a two-person trip.
